We’ll Save You A Seat

Visiting a new church for the first time can feel overwhelming, but at JCAM Church, we want you to feel at home from the moment you arrive. Our team is here to make your experience welcoming and stress-free!

Plan your visit today, and we will:

  • Welcome you at the entrance with a friendly smile
  • Introduce you to our pastors and church family
  • Assist with checking in your children for our Kids Ministry
  • Give you a tour of our church facilities
  • Reserve a special seat for you in our worship service

We can’t wait to meet you and share a meaningful time together!
